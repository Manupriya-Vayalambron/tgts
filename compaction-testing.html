About Compaction & Strength Testing

Compaction and strength testing are critical for determining the engineering properties of soil that directly impact foundation design, pavement construction, and earthwork projects. Our laboratory provides accurate and reliable test results following IS and ASTM standards.

Proper compaction testing ensures that soil achieves the required density for supporting structures, while strength tests determine the soil's ability to withstand loads without failure.
