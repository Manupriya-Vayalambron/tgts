About Field Investigation

A thorough field investigation is the foundation of any successful construction project. Our team provides comprehensive on-site support for soil sampling, in-situ testing, and subsurface exploration to help you understand the ground conditions at your project site.

We work closely with contractors, architects, and structural engineers to ensure that the right samples are collected and tested for your specific project requirements.

Our Services

Soil Sampling

Disturbed and undisturbed soil sample collection at various depths

Trial Pit Excavation

Shallow exploration with visual soil profiling

Bore Hole Logging

Detailed documentation of subsurface strata

In-Situ Density Test

Field density by sand replacement and core cutter methods

Plate Load Test

Bearing capacity determination at site

Standard Penetration Test

SPT for soil strength correlation
