About Our Soil Testing Lab

Our state-of-the-art soil testing laboratory is equipped with modern instruments and follows IS (Indian Standards) and ASTM protocols for all tests. We provide accurate and reliable soil characterization to support foundation design and construction decisions.

With over 10 years of experience, our team of qualified geotechnical engineers and technicians ensure that every sample is tested with precision and the results are delivered on time.

Tests We Offer

Grain Size Analysis

Sieve analysis and hydrometer test for particle size distribution

Atterberg Limits

Liquid limit, plastic limit, and plasticity index determination

Moisture Content

Natural moisture content and specific gravity tests

Soil Classification

IS/USCS classification based on test results

Permeability Test

Constant head and falling head permeability tests

Consolidation Test

One-dimensional consolidation for settlement analysis
